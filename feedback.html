After consulting with my teammates about my E-learning Social Media Platform project. Mr. Dang Vu Thang which my team leader has suggested me about building my platform mobile device. He said that at the current, there are many people use mobile to access the internet to enter the social media platform or even online study. Because he is an expert in developing website and already had some experiences with building social network on smartphone so he can provide me section how to build mobile application. With his feedback, I have found a Google report that 40% of Vietnamese users use mobile devices to access social media to communicate. I do agree with his suggestion because I do also believe in the potential of smartphones in providing convenient access and easy communication for the users. Other two members who are Chi and Nhi also give me a similar feedback idea. For them, good website design with social trendy arts can entertain and attract more potential customers like Generation Z and millennials. They believe that with e-learning platforms, it will be boring if the design and content show only about the education theme. So, they want to change a little about the design to make it more attractive. 43% people search the entertaining topics on YouTube (Google). This data shows that, if my platform wants to attract more users, trendy and entertaining designs can be implemented. I think I agree with their feedback.
