Team Name

Teamwork is when people on a team can show off their personalities, abilities, and talents to contribute to the group's project. I spent a lot of time looking for team name. And finally, I came up with a name that can encourage all members to come up with their unique characteristics which is The Tech-Unique. The “Unique” is a meaningful word which can influence and inspire all members to enjoy and contribute all their best to the project with their specific personalities. “The true value of a person is not in the IPad or IPhone, but Iam”.

Team Profile

In my team, except for me as a debater who is extraverted, intuitive, thinking and prospecting, my other team members are all mediators. I need them because I need their feeling and emotion in doing the jobs which they can be very creative instead of logical thinking like me. By combining logical thinking and feelings, I think our team can bring up an extremely creative project idea which can be sure about using both rational and emotional appeal to capture the user’s intention. At the beginning, I was afraid because they are introverted, they talk less, but after trying to build on relationship with them, I saw that they are quite opened. I am sure that my team could do the best in contributing hard work into the project.
